The landing page

The landing page was designed to be really showy - it uses a striking opening image to show that the website is professional while also being friendly. This is achieved by the flowing components:

  • The opening sentence, “Hi!” makes the reader understand the tone of the website.
  • The parallax effect on the opening page shows off my computing ability and makes the website seem more professional.
  • The shrinking of the ‘G. T. Flint’ logo also shows off my computing ability as well as smoothly transitioning to the content of the website. This makes it far easier to explore further into the website.
